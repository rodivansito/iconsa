About us

In 2006, Rodrigo Eisenmann and Javier Ferrer establish Ingeniería Continental S.A. (ICONSA) as a first-class engineering and construction firm, incorporating a team of engineers and experienced professionals. ICONSA specializes in the areas of engineering and construction that represent technical challenges and logistics: Marine Works, Foundations, Structural and Geotechnical Solutions, Civil Works and Industrial Buildings and Road Infrastructure.

Certifications

The Senior Management establishes its commitment to comply with the quality management system according to the ISO 9001: 2015 standard and to continuously improve the processes considering the risks inherent in the operations and complying with the legal and regulatory requirements. To achieve this goal, the company will rely on qualified professionals with extensive experience, committed and aware that quality is a right of the client.
